Status: Not Started

## Current Component

- `components/sections/home/shop-section.tsx`

## Proposed Replacement

- Prefer 21st.dev `Product Card` as the primary replacement direction.
- Keep the existing product content map, categories, prices, color swatches, and placeholder save/cart behavior.
- Decide during implementation whether to preserve the current CSS product-art system or replace cards with image-led product media.
- Use Magic UI `magic-card` only as a fallback if the product card structure stays mostly custom.

## Why It Was Chosen

Shop cards need ecommerce-specific structure, not only hover polish. 21st.dev `Product Card` is a better semantic fit because it includes product media, price, badge, wishlist, variant, and cart patterns that map directly to this section.

## Estimated Implementation Complexity

High

## Dependencies

- 21st.dev `Product Card`
- Package dependency likely required by the selected implementation: `framer-motion`
- Existing shadcn `Card`, `Button`, and `Badge`
- Existing `lucide-react` icons

## Are Motion Animations Alone Sufficient?

No. The product card structure, cart/save placeholders, swatches, and accessible product controls require component work beyond Motion-only polish.

## Acceptance Notes

- Category chips remain deterministic until real filtering is implemented.
- Product cards must not become visually noisy.
- Save behavior remains `[PLACEHOLDER]` until account/favorites state exists.
