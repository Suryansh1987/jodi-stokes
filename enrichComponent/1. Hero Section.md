## Why It Was Chosen

21st.dev `PulseFit hero` is fitness-specific and gives a richer hero direction than Magic UI `hero-video-dialog`. It includes program-card motion and fitness imagery patterns, but it should be adapted rather than copied wholesale because it also includes its own nav and light palette.

## Estimated Implementation Complexity

High

## Dependencies

- 21st.dev `PulseFit hero`
- `framer-motion`
- Existing `next/image` assets should replace remote sample images.
- Optional fallback: Magic UI `hero-video-dialog` with `motion`.

## Are Motion Animations Alone Sufficient?

No. Motion is part of the visual effect, but the actual work is adapting a full fitness hero to the existing content model, image assets, and reference layout.
