Status: Not Started  
Mandatory: Yes  
Depends On: Tasks 0-2

## Goal

Implement the first-viewport brand experience and introductory content from the mock.

## Reference Mock Findings

- Hero headline: `That's it, be fitt. Train with Jodi.`
- Hero has a badge, two CTAs, three stats, portrait image, caption, play button, and two floating result/method chips.
- Pillars section contains three equal columns: Trainer, Lifestyle, Nutrition.
- About section uses `jodi-leadership.png`, signature badge, two paragraphs, and four credentials.

## Implementation Scope

- Create:
  - `components/sections/home/hero-section.tsx`
  - `components/sections/home/pillars-section.tsx`
  - `components/sections/home/about-section.tsx`
- Use `next/image` for real images.
- Use CSS/Tailwind to preserve the mock's dark editorial layout and responsive behavior.
- Use `lucide-react` icons for CTA arrows, play, checkmarks, and pillar arrows where appropriate.
- Add accessible alt text for all images.
- Keep the hero image visually inspectable, not overly dark or blurred.
- Mark intro video action with `[PLACEHOLDER]` and future video modal with `[TODO]`.

## Files To Inspect

- First viewport clearly communicates Jodi Stokes Fitness.
- Hero CTA anchors work.
- Hero stats and floating chips remain readable on desktop and do not overlap on mobile.
- Pillars stack cleanly on small screens.
- About credentials render in a stable two-column desktop and one-column mobile layout.
